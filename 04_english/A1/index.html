    • Personal Information
    • Do NOT say "I to be"
    • Am, Is (She, He, It) Are (You, We, They)
    • A men, a woman or it (is)
    • Positive S + TB + Compl
    • Negative S + Am/Is/Are + Not + Compl
    • Interrogative Am/Is/Are + S + Compl?
